saxaphonist

This is the first project I made after deciding to do character animation. The project aims to capture a street artist playing the saxophone at a metro station.

The production of the project took 1 month. I can say that I worked hard for it to take 1 month. As someone who tends to prolong things, I made a special effort in this project to avoid that. I also think that I have been successful.

Although I see quite a few shortcomings in the project when I look at it now, I am very satisfied with the final result, or should I say "the final saxophone" :D
